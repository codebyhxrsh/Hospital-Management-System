Departments in a Hospital

Hospital is an integration of several departments such as clinical, nursing, supportive, technical, and administrative. Each department has different purposes and requirements. The list of different departments is as follows -

Clinical Departments
  • Casualty
  • Operating theatre (OT)
  • Cardiology
  • ENT
Supportive Departments
  • Pharmacy
  • Radiology
  • Clinical pathology
Technical Departments
  • Clinical engineering
  • Information technology
Administrative Departments
  • Medical records
  • Human resources
